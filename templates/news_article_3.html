September 15, 2023

CHARLOTTE, NORTH CAROLINA – September 15, 2023 – Chip Stock, a factory direct electronic component distributor and supply chain solution service, today announced it has been named as one of the Top 20 Global Independent Distributors in Electronics Sourcing North America's 2023 report.

Electronics Sourcing North America (ESNA) annually recognizes independent distributors who demonstrate excellence in sourcing, quality assurance, customer service, and overall supply chain performance. Being named to this prestigious list is a testament to Chip Stock's ongoing commitment to delivering reliable, traceable, and high-quality components to manufacturers across the globe.

"We are honored to be recognized among the top global independent distributors," said Dustin Osborne, President of Chip Stock. "This recognition is a direct result of the dedication our team shows every day — going above and beyond to solve supply chain challenges for our customers, no matter how complex."

As component shortages and supply chain disruptions continue to challenge manufacturers across industries, Chip Stock has positioned itself as a trusted partner for procurement teams seeking fast, reliable access to both standard and hard-to-find electronic components.

Chip Stock's approach combines factory-direct sourcing, a globally connected supplier network, and rigorous quality verification to ensure every part delivered meets or exceeds customer specifications. Their services span shortage sourcing, obsolete and end-of-life component procurement, excess inventory management, and full testing and inspection services.

For more information, visit ChipStock.com.
