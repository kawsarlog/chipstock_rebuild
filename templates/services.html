Testing & Quality Verification

At Chipstock, quality isn't just a checkbox — it's a non-negotiable standard. Every component we handle goes through a rigorous multi-step verification process to ensure authenticity, functionality, and compliance. We adhere to AS6081 and IDEA-STD-1010 guidelines, partnering with certified third-party labs to perform detailed inspections, including visual analysis, dimensional checks, X-ray and XRF testing, blacktop and remarking detection, decapsulation, and functional electrical testing when required.
