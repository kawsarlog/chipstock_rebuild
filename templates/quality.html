Our Quality Process

Testing & Compliance

At Chipstock, every component we deliver undergoes a strict testing and compliance process to ensure it meets both industry standards and your specific requirements. We partner with certified third-party testing labs to perform all inspections, ensuring objectivity and eliminating any potential bias. This independent validation allows us to guarantee that every part we ship is authentic, functional, and fully traceable.

Our compliance checks include verifying manufacturer specifications, packaging condition, labeling accuracy, and date code consistency. We adhere to AS6081 and IDEA-STD-1010 protocols to safeguard your supply chain from counterfeit or non-conforming components — providing peace of mind, documentation, and quality you can trust.

Component Verification

Component verification is an essential process in the electronics industry, ensuring the authenticity, reliability, and functionality of electronic components. At ChipStock, a comprehensive approach to quality control is applied to eliminate the risk of counterfeit or defective parts, protecting the integrity of electronic systems.

Through advanced inspection techniques, components undergo detailed analysis, including visual inspection, dimensional verification, and electrical testing to confirm their electrical and chemical properties. Cutting-edge methods such as X-ray imaging and material analysis help detect hidden defects, inconsistencies, or unauthorized modifications. These measures ensure that all components comply with industry standards and function as intended.

Advanced Analysis

For components requiring deeper validation, Chipstock offers advanced analysis services that go beyond standard inspection to confirm internal structure, material composition, and electrical performance. This level of testing is essential for high-reliability applications, legacy systems, and any situation where absolute confidence in component integrity is critical.

Our advanced testing capabilities include X-ray imaging, XRF material analysis, decapsulation and die verification, solderability testing, and electrical functionality checks. These processes are designed to detect counterfeits, verify construction, and ensure every part meets or exceeds performance expectations.

Final Quality Approval

As part of our commitment to final quality, every component goes through a final quality approval process before it's cleared for shipment. This final checkpoint ensures that all parts have passed inspection, advanced testing, and documentation review according to our strict quality standards. Our team verifies packaging integrity, labeling accuracy, moisture barrier seals, and shipment readiness — making sure every order leaves our facility in full compliance.

We don't just stand by our process — we back it. Once a component has met all approval criteria, it receives final sign-off and ships with a Certificate of Conformance. Every part you receive is covered by our 1-year warranty.
